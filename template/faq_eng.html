Is this a financial pyramid?

This is not a financial pyramid. In all these schemes money is accumulated at the top of the pyramid, in the hands of big bosses. When the pyramid has made enough money these big bosses usually disappear. We don not have actual top structure, we even do not possess any internal payment system.

All payments involve only two people: the supervisor and his/her client, there is no any other third person who gets his/her commission.

We use the traditional trade scheme — "when good is exchanged for money." In our system the program levels act as goods.If skeptics think it's nonsense, let them believe it further more. Program levels are the most important goods in our project.

And finally: "O glorious pyramid, I'm afraid to spell your name…" My dear, we are living in the 21th century but the network marketing only begins its way in the strata of our society. All big companies are moving towards partnership programs. But each partnership program is nothing but a kind of network marketing. You attract clients (buyers), for this activity the organization gives you a percentage, for example from each deal you have made. Lots of these programs present a multilevel scheme so that with every next level you get smaller percent. Do you have any idea why you get less? The answer is simple: the company just doesn't want to share its profit, they invent rules of 70% and etc. Now please estimate your role in our project. You are an organization (firm) or a successful trader yourself and get 100% profit from your own sales. That's perfect.

How the payment between partners is made?

All payments are held between the client and his/her supervisor. The order for the purchase of the next level is made in the Personal Office, then the client receives the supervisor's data necessary for money transition. No other person is involved in the process of purchase and sale. So that everything is organized according to the rules of marketing plan.

  2. During 72 hours you are to take the decision to become the participant of our project and buy the first level of program from your supervisors;
  3. Advertise you reference link (for example just post it in some forums);
  4. You see that you link is used by new visitors and those who get interested in it will register in you network;
  5. You see that your network is growing fast, that means that your business works;
  6. Everyday you check your e-mail box and sell levels of the program "MoneyLand" to your clients and your business starts bringing you money.
That's unreal! Organizers should get some hidden profits.

The project activity is absolutely transparent and you can see what each participants is involved into and how many clients he/she has. We don't have any internal payment system. All participants work in one and the same conditions and all of them possess equal rights. Each participant can have only 3 levels of clients, those who have below level of the program are not connected with those who have beyond level of it.

The group of organizers consists of the same participants who control the work of the whole system, do administrative work and answer your questions. More then that, they do it on free terms as nobody pays them. So it's quite clear that the best and less laborious position is a position of an ordinary user of the system.
