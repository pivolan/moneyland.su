Liqpay

LiqPay is a payment system which enables you to send money with your VISA or MasterCard to the Liqpay account which is attributed to the cell phone number.

Once you have done it you may transfer the money to another cell phone or withdraw the money from the system or just keep your money at the account and use it when necessary.

LiqPay is the best option if you need to send money to the person and you have no other information except for his/her cell phone number. The way it works is this. The receiver just gets SMS offering him/her to withdraw the money with LiqPay and he/she does not have to be registered in the system. If he/she needs to transfer money to his/her plastic card or send it further he/she needs to login to the LiqPay site which will make other transactions possible.

LiqPay offers a smart way to handle micro payments in the Internet. The minimal amount of money for a transaction is 0,01 of the currency unit. What makes Liqpay attractive is that they charge possibly the lowest fees of all payment systems.

What card do you need to have to use LiqPay?

You need to have Visa or MasterCard issued by any bank which has no restrictions for the Internet transactions. In order to accomplish a complete withdrawal of the money from the system you have to have Visa card. The reason for this is that Master Card has not made it possible to receive money from other banks so far. As far as Russia is concerned, LiqPay does not work with Sberbank.

What cell phone providers does LiqPay work with?

LiqPay works with all major cell phone providers around the world.

What currency does LiqPay work with?

Liqpay works with Euro, US dollar, Russian rouble and Ukrainian hryvna. The system is going to develop further and introduce all major world currencies in the turnover. The system performs an automatic currency convertion if the currency of the card and the currency of LiqPay account do not coincide.
