User agreement

This agreement does not have any legal force, however it presents the list of rules according to which the whole team is working. If a client violates the rules, he/she may be banned from using our resource.

1. On the entrance step the client has a right:

1.1. To register in the project “MoneyLand” according to his/her will and try the system in test terms. The time of test is limited and it comprises of7 2 hours from the moment of registration. During the time of testing he/she receives the status of “non-activated participant” (the client who undergoes the process of registration but acquires the “non-activated” status means that he/she can not sell levels of program “MoneyLand” and activate new participants;

1.2. To pay and be activated on the site during 72 hours after registration. Non-activated within 72 hours client will be deleted from the project without his/her consent.

2. The following financial relations are established between the participants of this project.

2.1. Under financial relations is understood:

a) transition of money from the client to his/her supervisor;

b) acceptance of money by the supervisor.

2.2. The cost of the first level of the program “MoneyLand” is 20 euro (this sum is distributed between supervisors of three lines);

2.3. The administration takes the responsibility of controlling the financial relations between the supervisor and his/her client but only after the presentation of complaint from any of two sides with the evidence of the current agreement violation.

3. Client's activation.

3.1. Having purchased the first level of the program “MoneyLand” user acquires the status of “activated participant” (full member of the system entering the business as a registered participant of the project who paid for the first level of the system). The responsibility of the authenticity of the status “activated participant” is laid upon the supervisors of three upper lines.

4. Rights and duties of activated participants.

4.1. The activated participant has a right:

a) use all presented services for doing his/her own business;

b) to ask administration for help in managing his/her business or for getting answers on some questions.

4.2. The activated participant is obliged:

a) to answer all messages from clients delivered to his address not later then within 24 hours from the moment of delivery;

b) when the client transfers the whole amount of money (equal to the cost of the first level) to the accounts of the supervisors of three lines), supervisors should activate him/her in Personal Office not later then within 24 hours after delivering the letter from the client;

c) to purchase the next level after the closing of the current level by activated participants within 72 hours;

d) the participant is not obliged to tell anyone paroles, codes and any other secret data provided by the system if it is not stated in the current agreement.

5. Communication rules and business etiquette.

5.1. The participants of the project should share business and partners relationship. The following actions concerning the participants of the project are prohibited:

a) the usage of unprintable and indecent words;

b) the deliberate usage of wrong information according to personal selfish ends;

c) other harm-doing.

6. Rights and obligations of administration.

6.1. Administration is obliged:

a) to fulfill the current agreement;

b) to provide the access to the program in accordance with the current agreement;

c) not to disfigure or change the user's data in the resource;

d) not to disclose the user's data to other people without his/her agreement;

e) to eliminate defects in proper time;

f) to investigate internal rules violation and inform about it;

g) take to consideration all complaints.

6.2. Administration has a right:

a) to change the text of the current agreement;

b) to ban the access to the system for the user who violates the current agreement. The decision of administration depends on the type of violation;

c) to modernize the resource.

7. The procedure of complaint presentation and its consideration.

7.1. The participant can complain to administration in case his/her rights, regulated by the current agreement, are violated. The participant should provide necessary proofs.

7.2. After presentation of proofs, administration connects with the person about whom the complaint is made and the case is investigated on bilateral basis.

7.3. In case of different violations administration presents its report in news block.
